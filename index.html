About Us
Built on Precision, Driven by Trust
Two decades of engineering footwear moulds that set industry benchmarks.
Est. 2000
Craftsmanship Meets Engineering

Founded by Sheikh Sikander, S.S. Classic Mould & Dies has been a cornerstone of India's footwear manufacturing ecosystem for over 25 years. Based in the heart of Delhi's industrial hub — Mangol Puri — we specialize in precision-engineered moulds that power some of India's most recognized footwear brands.

Every mould that leaves our workshop carries our commitment to dimensional accuracy, surface finish quality, and long-lasting durability. We don't just manufacture moulds — we engineer solutions that help our clients scale production with confidence.

From Concept to Delivery
A streamlined, transparent workflow designed around your production schedule.
01

Enquiry & Consultation

Share your requirements — product type, size range, volume, and timeline. Our team reviews feasibility and provides a detailed quotation within 24 hours.

02

Design & Engineering

Our CAD team creates precise 3D mould designs based on your specifications. You receive digital previews and detailed technical drawings for approval.

Manufacturing

CNC machining, EDM, and hand finishing — every mould goes through our rigorous multi-stage production process with in-process quality checks.
